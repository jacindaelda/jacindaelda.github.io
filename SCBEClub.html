About SCBE Club

SCBE Club (School of Chemical and Biomedical Engineering Club) organizes events which improve the welfare of students, as well as which engages both students and staffs of SCBE. These events include SCBE Week, SCBE Inter-Year Games, Old Textbook Sales, PYP Solution Distribution, and SCBE Slurpz (Beauty Pageant). For more information about SCBE Club, click this link.

SCBE Week

SCBE Week is held one month before exam period. It is an annual event which gives students an opportunity to relieve stress by playing mini games and earning free-gifts. In addition to mini-games, there is also SCBE Talent Night which showcases SCBE students' talents on stage. Moreover, free dinner is also provided to accompany SCBE students and staff while they are enjoying the performances on stage. There is also free goodie-bags distributed for SCBE students as a way for the club to support them on the upcoming final exams.

SCBE Inter-Year Games

Inter-Year Games (IYG) is an annual sports competition which aims to improve the friendship among SCBE students, and also the relationship between the students and faculty staff through sportsmanship and fellowship. Moreover, this event also allows students to showcase their talent in sports and earn rewards.
